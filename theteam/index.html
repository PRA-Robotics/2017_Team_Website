The Team

Our team is organized into three subteams or "departments". These teams are hardware, software, and organization. Each of these departments have been assigned aspects of the competition that are critical to the team. The hardware team works on the construction of the physical robot itself. The Software team works on the programs for both teleop and autonomous functions of the robot. The Organization team works on the aspects of the robot that are not necessarily part of the game. Each team has a Co-Captain that they have chosen to organize their particular department. They help organize the departments and ensure that the tasks given to their department are completed. Finally, our team elects a Team Captain to act as the leader of the entire team. With this organization we hope to build an efficient team that can be a true competitor in the First Tech Challenge.
