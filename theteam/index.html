Roboticsisms

Our team is made up of Freshman, Sophomores, and Juniors. Our school is still very new and does not have a Senior class yet. Coming from a student body that would cause anyone to call it a "nerd school", our team really doesn't stand a chance. Every day is filled with playful banter, cheesy jokes, and a healthy dose of slapstick humor. With every personality fluctuating between being responsible and having an attention span of a flea, many amusing moments emerge from daily conversations.

Despite what can sometimes be an amusingly chaotic environment, our team still manages to bring a robot together throughout the season. Having the different departments of the team work smoothly together and accomplish tasks on schedule with other groups is a daunting task, yet we bring all of our efforts together to complete a functional robot. The interactions can be difficult and can sometimes reach the boiling point, but the comradery of the team ensures that we overcome any speed bumps in the process and complete the tasks at hand. As a team, we have come together to construct a robot that can be a real competitor in the First Tech Challenge.
