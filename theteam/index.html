The Team: Why FIRST®?

Before Prospect Ridge Academy had even added a high school, the middle school began a FIRST® Lego League Team. Then, after students began to request a high school robotics team, and because of the positive experience we'd had with FIRST® in the middle school, the high school elected to host a FIRST® Tech Challenge Team.
