Welcome

Team 8969, A.K.A. Control-Alt-Defeat, is a team from Prospect Ridge Academy in Broomfield, Colorado. The 2016-2017 season, Velocity Vortex, was our 3rd season as a First Tech Challenge participant.

The purpose of this website is to provide details about our team as well as explain many of the methods we use to accomplish the tasks we are faced with. Feel free to look around and learn about our team and its members as well as how we do what we do.
