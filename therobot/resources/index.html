Resources

As a team, we run under the general rule that if something can be improved, improve it. Because of this, we feel that we have come up with some tips, tricks, and resources of our own that we would like to share with anyone interested. Below, you will find many of the resources we have utilized and that we have to offer.

External Resources

As we build and maintain this website, we try to provide as much detail as you would like pertaining to our team. However, we don't want to crowd the pages with information, which means we will be happy to summarize several aspects of the general competition, but we will focus our efforts on describing our team rather than the competition. So, we have provided several links to different pages on the FIRST® Tech Challenge website and any other sites we feel may provide the information you desire.
