The Robot: Giving Life to Metal

Many people believe that only smart people can build robots. We find that although having the know-how up front helps, it is more a matter of taking the time to learn. Anyone willing to invest the time, effort, and a few hundred dollars for materials will be able to construct a robot to do anything. The problem is, if no one teaches, no one can learn. So we've decided that since we like to think that we know what we are doing, we'll go ahead and give your our two cents on how to build a robot.
