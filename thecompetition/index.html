The Competition: FIRST® RELIC RECOVERYSM

FIRST® RELIC RECOVERYSM is a competition with ancient temple themed tasks such as arranging glyphs to make images or, as the name suggests, recovering relics. This particular year appears at first glance to be less of a direct competition between alliances and more of a time trial. With this in mind, we are looking forward to planning a building our robot this year.

Challenge Overview

This year's challenge is fairly unique as there aren't any major elements recycled from past challenges. With the exception of the "jewels", which are the particles from last year, every game element is new to us. This means we have a real challenge ahead of us this year. But enough about us, here are the basic details of the competition. Each match is played by four, randomly selected teams split into two alliances. The goal of each match is to score as many points as you can for your alliance by performing tasks unique to each season. Different tasks offer different point values, so teams often have to choose between different tasks as they plan their robot, leaving some points unscored. The alliance with the most points at the end of the match wins, and both teams on the alliance are awarded qualifying points.
