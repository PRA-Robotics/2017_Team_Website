Challenge Overview

This year's challenge is fairly unique as there aren't any major element recycled from past challenges. With the exception of the "jewels", which are the particles from last year, every game element is new to this us. This means we have a real challenge this year ahead of us this year. But enough about us, here are the basic details of the competition. Each match is played by four, randomly selected teams split into two alliances. The goal of each match is to score as many points as you can for your alliance by performing tasks unique to each season. Different tasks offer different point values, so teams often have to choose between different tasks as they plan their robot, leaving some points unscored. The alliance with the most points at the end of the match wins, and both teams on the alliance are awarded qualifying points.
